# Car Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview  
This project is a Car Sales Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze sales performance across different dimensions such as time, region, vehicle attributes, and dealerships. The goal is to provide clear insights into revenue trends, customer preferences, and overall business performance.

## Key KPIs  
- YTD Total Sales: $371.2M  
- YTD Cars Sold: 13.3K  
- Average Price: $28.0K  
- Sales Growth: +23.59%  
- Cars Sold Growth: +19.73%  

## Dashboard Highlights  
- Weekly sales trend showing performance fluctuations and peak periods  
- Sales breakdown by body style and color to understand customer preferences  
- Regional analysis highlighting top-performing locations  
- Company-wise comparison of sales and average pricing  
- Detailed transaction view for deeper analysis  

## Key Insights  
- Strong growth in both revenue and sales volume  
- Slight decline in average price suggests pricing or product mix changes  
- SUVs and Hatchbacks are the most preferred vehicle types  
- Neutral colors like white and black dominate sales  
- Some regions and dealers significantly outperform others  

## Tools Used  
- Power BI  
- Power Query  
- DAX
